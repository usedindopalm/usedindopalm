Company Overview | 公司简介

We are an Indonesian exporter and supplier of Used Cooking Oil (UCO) for bioenergy, renewable fuel, industrial boiler and power plant applications.

Our company focuses on providing stable supply, consistent quality, and competitive pricing to support global renewable energy industries.

With a reliable sourcing network and strict quality control system, we serve international buyers, especially in China and other Asian markets.
